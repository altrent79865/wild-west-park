Boise’s Western-Themed Family Park

Round up the crew for rope bridges, splash zones, giant slides and events—  free all day.

Gold Mountain Rush

Why Families Love It

  • Always Free: Open daily. No admission costs.
  • Designed for All Ages: Fun for everyone.
  • Accessible: Ramps, adaptive swings and more.
  • Nature + Play: Stroll through the gardens and explore winding trails.
  • Community Hub: Storytimes, live music and seasonal festivals.

Explore the Park

Gold Mountain Rush

Experience fun-filled slides that wind down red rock slopes. Glide past the entrance to a gold mine and behind a waterfall.

Pioneer Town

Climb up to the bank’s second floor, cross the rope bridge to the hotel, and zip down the swift slide.

Frontier Towers

Scale the towers, venture onto the rope bridges, and whisk down twisty slides. A zip-line offers big-kid thrills.

Silver Spur Splash Yard

Splash in the water jets, send water rushing down sluice channels, and pan for gold. Features non-slip surfaces. (Seasonal)

Little Homesteaders

Younger children can climb aboard the locomotive and slide down its gentle slide. The little ones will enjoy the climbers, musical panels and bucket swings.

Prairie Gardens

Unwind on the porch-style swings in the beautifully landscaped gardens.

Plan Your Visit

  • Location A short drive from downtown Boise.
  • Hours Open daily, dawn to dusk.
  • Parking On-site lot, bike racks and ADA parking.
  • Restrooms Family restrooms. Water refill stations.
  • Food Picnic spots, BBQ grills and weekend food trucks.
  • Accessibility Smooth paths, ramped structures and adaptive swings.

Events & Programs

  • Music in the Park (summer): Local artists, open-air concerts.
  • Learn Frontier Skills: Pioneer crafts, knot-tying, cooking classes.
  • Starlight Cinema (summer): Outdoor family movies at dusk.
  • Volunteer Saturdays: Garden tending and trail cleanups.

Safety & Park Etiquette

  • Trained park hosts during peak hours.
  • Shaded structures, temperature-tested surfaces, impact-safe materials.
  • Please always supervise your children.
  • Service animals welcome.

FAQs

Is it really free?

Yes—admission and most programs are free. Some special events may have optional concessions.

Are there age limits?

Zones are marked by recommended ages (2–5, 5–12, 12+), but families can explore together.

What about weather closures?

We’re open in most conditions barring inclement weather. The splash yard and some structures are open seasonally.
